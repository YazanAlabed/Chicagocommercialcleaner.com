Chicago offices face unique challenges. The Loop high-rises bring city dust through lobby doors. Winter tracks in salt from November through March. Summer humidity affects carpets. Your keyboard alone has 400x more bacteria than a toilet seat — and that's before anyone sneezes.

Most Chicago offices get surface-level cleaning. Carpets vacuumed. Trash emptied. Counters wiped. The things employees actually touch all day? Keyboards. Door handles. Elevator buttons. The break room sink. These get skipped more often than you'd think.
