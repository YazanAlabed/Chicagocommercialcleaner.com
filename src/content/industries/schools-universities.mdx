### Why School Cleaning Matters

Schools spread illness fast. One sick child touches a desk, a door handle, a water fountain. Within hours, germs move through classrooms. By the time teachers notice, half the class might be coughing.

Flu season hits Chicago schools hard from November through March. The right cleaning reduces absenteeism. Schools cleaned 3x/week see 46% fewer sick days according to ISSA research. That translates to better learning and fewer make-up days.
