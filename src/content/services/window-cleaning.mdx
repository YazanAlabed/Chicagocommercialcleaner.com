### Why Do Windows Need Regular Cleaning?

**TL;DR:** Chicago weather (salt, pollution, pollen) creates buildup on windows. Dirty windows affect building appearance, employee morale, and natural light. Regular cleaning prevents permanent damage to glass.

Chicago windows face specific challenges: Salt from winter roads. Pollution from traffic. Pollen in spring. Construction dust. Industrial fallout. These create buildup that etches glass over time.

Dirty windows reduce natural light by up to 20%. They affect employee productivity and morale. They create a poor impression on clients and visitors.
