## Janitorial Services for Chicago Businesses

**Quick Answer:** Janitorial services in Chicago cost $0.12-$0.30 per square foot per month, with most businesses paying $500-$2,000 monthly for daily maintenance. Services include trash removal, floor care, restroom cleaning, and surface maintenance.

### What Do Janitorial Services Include?

**TL;DR:** Daily tasks like vacuuming, mopping, trash removal, restroom cleaning, break room maintenance, and surface wiping. Monthly deep cleaning and special projects are available separately.

**Daily Tasks:**
- Vacuum all carpeted areas
- Mop hard floor surfaces
- Empty trash and recycling bins
- Clean and restock restrooms
- Wipe break room surfaces
- Spot clean spills and marks
- Dust horizontal surfaces

**Weekly Tasks:**
- Thorough dusting of all surfaces
- Clean glass entry doors
- Vacuum upholstered furniture
- Deep clean break room areas

**Monthly Options:**
- Carpet spot cleaning
- Hard floor buffing
- Window cleaning (interior)
- Deep restroom cleaning

### How Do Janitorial Services Differ from Commercial Cleaning?

**TL;DR:** Janitorial services are daily maintenance (trash, floors, restrooms). Commercial cleaning includes deeper services like carpet extraction, floor refinishing, and window washing. Most businesses need both.

**Janitorial Services = Daily Maintenance**
- Regular, scheduled cleaning
- Surface-level tasks
- Preventive maintenance
- Keeps facilities presentable

**Commercial Cleaning = Deep Cleaning**
- Periodic, project-based
- Carpet extraction
- Floor stripping and refinishing
- Window washing
- Deep sanitation

**Most businesses need both:**
Janitorial maintains daily cleanliness. Commercial cleaning restores what daily use wears down. Budget for monthly janitorial plus quarterly deep cleaning.

### Who Needs Janitorial Services?

**Offices**
Daily trash removal, restroom maintenance, floor care. Keeps workspaces professional and healthy.

**Retail Stores**
Floor care, restroom cleaning, entrance maintenance. Critical for customer-facing businesses.

**Medical Facilities**
Daily cleaning plus disinfection protocols. HIPAA-compliant practices for patient areas.

**Warehouses**
Floor scrubbing, break room maintenance, restroom cleaning. Larger scale, different equipment.

**Schools**
Daily cleaning of classrooms, restrooms, common areas. Focus on high-touch surfaces.

**Restaurants**
Kitchen deep cleaning, dining area maintenance, restroom sanitation. Health code compliance focus.
