## Commercial Floor Care for Chicago Businesses

**Quick Answer:** Floor stripping and waxing costs $0.50-$1.00 per square foot in Chicago. Buffing costs $0.05-$0.15 per square foot. Most floors need stripping 1-2 times per year with monthly buffing in between.

### Why Do Floors Need Regular Maintenance?

**TL;DR:** Dirt and grit act like sandpaper on floor finishes. Foot traffic wears down wax. Without regular maintenance, floors become dull, scratched, and damaged. Proper care extends floor life by 3-5 years.

VCT and hard floors take daily abuse. Foot traffic. Rolling chairs. Equipment. Salt in Chicago winters. Without maintenance, finishes wear through to the tile. Replacement costs $3-$5 per square foot. Regular maintenance costs a fraction of that.

### What Floor Care Services Do We Offer?

**TL;DR:** Floor stripping and waxing for VCT and vinyl. Hardwood floor refinishing. Concrete polishing and sealing. Buffing and spray buffing. Concrete floor care. Maintenance between deep services.

**Stripping and Waxing**
Complete removal of old wax and finish. Application of new wax coats. Most floors need 4-6 coats. Restores shine and protection.

**Buffing and Spray Buffing**
Buffing removes scuffs and restores shine between full waxes. Spray buffing adds a thin wax layer for added protection. Monthly or quarterly service.

**Hardwood Floor Care**
Screening and recoating for hardwood floors. Deep cleaning. Floor refinishing when needed. Hardwood requires different care than VCT.

**Concrete Floor Care**
Concrete polishing and sealing. Stain removal. Epoxy coating maintenance. Industrial concrete needs specialized care.

### How Often Should Floors Be Maintained?

**TL;DR:** High-traffic areas need stripping 2x per year with monthly buffing. Medium-traffic areas need stripping 1x per year with quarterly buffing. Low-traffic areas need stripping annually.

| Area | Stripping/Waxing | Buffing |
|------|-----------------|---------|
| Lobbies/entrances | 2x/year | Monthly |
| Hallways | 2x/year | Every 1-2 months |
| General offices | 1x/year | Quarterly |
| Low-traffic areas | Annually | As needed |
