**Quick Answer:** Post-construction cleaning in Chicago costs $0.30-$0.75 per square foot. It includes debris removal, dust removal from all surfaces, window cleaning, floor cleaning, and final detail work. Most projects take 1-3 days.

### Why Is Post-Construction Cleaning Different?

**TL;DR:** Construction dust is finer than household dust and gets everywhere—inside outlets, on light fixtures, in air vents. Regular cleaning doesn't remove it. You need industrial equipment and proper disposal methods.

Construction leaves behind more than new walls. Dust in every crevice. Debris in corners. Stickers on windows. Drywall particles in the air. The final step before move-in is cleaning — but not just any cleaning.

**What Makes It Different:**
- Construction dust is microscopic and penetrates surfaces
- Debris includes hazardous materials (nails, glass, chemicals)
- Adhesive residue from stickers and protective films
- Proper disposal required for construction debris

### What Does Post-Construction Cleaning Include?

**TL;DR:** Debris removal, dust removal from all surfaces, window cleaning (inside and out), floor cleaning, fixture cleaning, vent cleaning, and final detail walkthrough.

**Debris Removal**
Remove construction debris left behind. Drywall pieces. Packaging. Scraps. Anything the contractors didn't take.

**Dust Removal**
Dust every surface. Walls. Ceilings. Light fixtures. Outlets and switches. Baseboards. Window sills. Door frames. Every surface in every room.

**Window Cleaning**
Remove stickers from new windows. Clean interior and exterior glass. Clean window frames and tracks.

**Floor Cleaning**
Sweep and mop hard floors. Vacuum carpets. Deep clean if needed. Remove paint overspray, adhesive residue, and construction marks.

**Fixture Cleaning**
Clean light fixtures. Ceiling fans. Faucets. Cabinet hardware. Door handles. Everything that was installed during construction.

**Vent Cleaning**
Dust registers and returns. Clean vent covers. Construction dust collects in HVAC systems.

**Final Detail**
Baseboards. Door frames. Window tracks. The details that get missed. We check everything.

### How Long Does Post-Construction Cleaning Take?

**TL;DR:** Most projects take 1-3 days. New construction may take less time than renovation. Size, debris level, and finish quality affect timeline.

**Day 1: Rough Cleaning**
Remove large debris. Scrape floors. Remove stickers from windows and fixtures. Clean the obvious mess.

**Day 2: Detailed Cleaning**
Dust all surfaces. Clean walls and ceilings. Clean light fixtures. Clean windows inside and out. Clean floors thoroughly.

**Day 3: Touch-Up and Final**
Walk through the entire space. Check for missed spots. Clean areas where dust settled overnight. Final inspection.

### How Do You Clean Around New Finishes?

**TL;DR:** We use appropriate methods for each surface. Marble, hardwood, and specialty finishes need specific cleaning products and techniques. We ask about finishes before starting.

**Hardwood Floors:**
Use proper wood floor cleaners. Avoid excess water. Protect new finishes.

**Marble and Stone:**
Use pH-neutral cleaners. Avoid acidic products that etch stone.

**Stainless Steel:**
Use stainless steel cleaners. Remove adhesive residue without scratching.

**Glass and Mirrors:**
Clean with proper glass cleaners. Remove stickers and adhesive residue.

**Painted Surfaces:**
Dust carefully. Avoid scrubbing that damages new paint.
