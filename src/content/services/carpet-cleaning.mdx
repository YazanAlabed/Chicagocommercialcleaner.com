### Why Do Carpets Need Professional Cleaning?

**TL;DR:** Daily vacuuming removes surface dirt but leaves embedded dirt that damages carpet fibers. Professional extraction removes deep dirt, extends carpet life by 3-5 years, and improves indoor air quality.

Carpets act like filters. They trap dust, allergens, and pollutants. Over time, they become saturated. Regular vacuuming only removes surface dirt. Deep extraction removes embedded dirt that damages fibers.

**What Daily Vacuuming Misses:**
- Dirt embedded 1/4 inch into carpet pile
- Allergens trapped in carpet fibers
- Pollutants from foot traffic
- Chicago winter salt residue
- Oil and grease from shoes

### What Carpet Cleaning Methods Do We Use?

**TL;DR:** Hot water extraction (steam cleaning) for deep cleaning. Low-moisture encapsulation for maintenance between deep cleanings. Bonnet cleaning for interim maintenance. Method depends on carpet type and traffic level.

**Hot Water Extraction**
Most effective deep cleaning method. Hot water and cleaning solution injected into carpet, then extracted with dirt. Dries in 4-8 hours. Removes deep dirt and stains.

**Low-Moisture Encapsulation**
For maintenance between deep cleanings. Cleaning solution crystallizes dirt, which is vacuumed up. Dries in 1-2 hours. Good for interim maintenance.

**Bonnet Cleaning**
Surface cleaning for interim maintenance. Rotating pad absorbs surface dirt. Dries fast. Not deep cleaning.
