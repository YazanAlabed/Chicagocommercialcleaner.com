## Post-Construction Cleaning: What Chicago Projects Need

Construction leaves behind more than new walls. Dust in every crevice. Debris in corners. Stickers on windows. Drywall particles in the air. The final step before move-in is cleaning — but not just any cleaning.

**Quick Answer:** Post-construction cleaning in Chicago costs $0.30-$0.75 per square foot. It includes debris removal, dust removal from all surfaces, window cleaning, floor cleaning, and detail work. Most projects take 1-3 days depending on size and scope.

### Why Post-Construction Cleaning Is Different

Post-construction cleaning isn't regular cleaning. The dust from construction is finer than household dust. It gets everywhere — inside electrical outlets, on light fixtures, in air vents, behind baseboards. Regular cleaning doesn't touch it.

Construction debris is also hazardous. Nails. Glass shards. Drywall dust. Chemical residues from adhesives and paints. You need proper equipment, safety gear, and disposal methods.

### Types of Post-Construction Cleaning

**Rough Clean**
Happens during or immediately after construction. Removes large debris, scrapes floors, cleans up the worst of the mess. Often done by contractors before they leave.

**Final Clean**
The detailed cleaning that makes the space ready for occupancy. Dust removal, window cleaning, fixture cleaning, floor care. This is what we do.

**Touch-Up Clean**
A final walkthrough after the first cleaning. Catches missed spots, dust that settled, and any remaining debris. Usually done 24-48 hours after the final clean.

### What's Included

**Debris Removal**
Remove construction debris left behind. Drywall pieces. Packaging. Scraps. Anything the contractors didn't take.

**Dust Removal**
Dust every surface. Walls. Ceilings. Light fixtures. Outlets and switches. Baseboards. Window sills. Door frames. The dust from construction is everywhere.

**Window Cleaning**
Remove stickers from new windows. Clean interior and exterior glass. Clean window frames and tracks.

**Floor Cleaning**
Sweep and mop hard floors. Vacuum carpets. Deep clean if needed. Remove paint overspray, adhesive residue, and construction marks.

**Fixture Cleaning**
Clean light fixtures. Ceiling fans. Faucets. Cabinet hardware. Door handles. Everything that was installed during construction.

**Vent Cleaning**
Dust registers and returns. Clean vent covers. Construction dust collects in HVAC systems.

**Bathroom Detail**
Clean toilets, sinks, and fixtures. Remove labels and stickers. Clean mirrors. Polish chrome.

**Kitchen Detail**
Clean cabinets inside and out. Clean countertops. Clean appliances. Remove stickers from appliances and fixtures.

**Final Detail**
Baseboards. Door frames. Window tracks. The details that get missed. We check everything.

### Timeline and Process

**Day 1: Rough Cleaning**
Remove large debris. Scrape floors. Remove stickers from windows and fixtures. Clean the obvious mess.

**Day 2: Detailed Cleaning**
Dust all surfaces. Clean walls and ceilings. Clean light fixtures. Clean windows inside and out. Clean floors thoroughly.

**Day 3: Touch-Up and Final**
Walk through the entire space. Check for missed spots. Clean areas where dust settled overnight. Final inspection.

**Timeline Factors:**

- Level of finish (rough construction vs. high-end)

### Common Questions

**Should contractors clean up after themselves?**
Yes, but they often don't do it thoroughly. They remove large debris but leave dust, stickers, and residue. Budget for professional cleaning regardless of what contractors promise.

**Can I use the space before final cleaning?**
You can, but dust will get on everything. HVAC systems will spread dust throughout the space. It's better to wait until after final cleaning.

**What if there's damage from construction?**
We document any damage we find before we start cleaning. Damage isn't a cleaning problem — it's a contractor issue. We'll let you know if we see anything.

**Do you clean windows on high-rises?**
We clean interior windows. Exterior windows on high-rises require specialized equipment. We can coordinate window washing services or work with your building's existing contractor.
