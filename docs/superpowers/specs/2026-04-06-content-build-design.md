**Content Sections:**
1. **Quick Answer** (60 words max) — Direct answer with primary keyword
2. **About [Suburb]** — Real landmarks, business parks, streets, local economy
3. **Our [Suburb] Cleaning Services** — Links to relevant service pages
4. **Why [Suburb] Businesses Choose Us** — 3 local-specific reasons
5. **Areas We Serve in [Suburb]** — Specific streets/neighborhoods
6. **Nearby Locations** — Links to adjacent suburb pages
7. **CTA** — Quote link + phone number

### Writing Rules (from human-SEO-writing-guide.md)
- Short sentences (avg 12-15 words, max 25)
- Varied rhythm — mix short punchy (5-8 words) with longer (15-20 words)
- Real Chicago/suburb specifics — street names, landmarks, local knowledge
- No AI words (delve, tapestry, pivotal, unlock, transform, etc.)
- No banned phrases ("In today's fast-paced", "Let's dive in", etc.)
- Max 1 em dash per 500 words
- Start with a hook, not a definition
- End with practical next step, not generic CTA
